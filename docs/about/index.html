About Me

My Name is Terry Sagas and video games are my passion. Video games allow me to escape the real world and explore a world of imagination. They have changed my life for the better and for the worse (sometimes), through games I met new friends and get closer to family. On my free time I play video games, mainly Nintendo games, like replaying a Zelda game or playing a new game just released. Other games I play are card games like Marvel Snap, a fast-paced card battler I can play on my phone and computer. A game that I played for a very long time is Destiny 2; I love that game. At the moment I am not playing, but I will find myself back into the game at some point. Without games, I may be a smarter person or someone who’s better at school, but I wouldn’t be as happy, know the people I do, and be as fun as I am today. Possibly in the future, I want to get into game development. Video games are my passion, and here is just a small website showing what games/game consoles I own.
